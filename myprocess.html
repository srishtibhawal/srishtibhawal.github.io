my steps



  1. Prep
    1. I wash my hands around 10-15 minutes before I start application to reduce moisture on my nails.
    2. I like to put on some music or a show while doing my nails!
    3. Before starting, I wipe my nails with Isopropyl Alcohol to remove grease and put on my gloves.
    4. I apply Cuticle Remover and wait 5-10 minutes before using a Cuticle Pusher to push back my cuticles.
    5. Sometimes I use a Cuticle Trimmer to cut my cuticles if they are too prominent.
    6. I then cut the tips of my nails to a shorter length (max 1-2 mm from my fingertip) and shape them using nail files.
    7. I also buff the top of my nails with my buffers.
  2. Application
    1. I use my nail drill to drill the inside of my nail tips, making them thinner and also textured which is better for application.
    2. Once everything is ready, I put a layer of foundation//base gel onto my nails, as well as a small amount on the tip.
    3. 3. I then place the nail under my UV lamp and cure it for 60-120 seconds.
    4. 4. Once all 5 are applied, I cure them for 100 seconds in my big lamp.
    5. Depending on the design of the set, I them clip my nails and repeat the shaping process.
